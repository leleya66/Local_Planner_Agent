Agent tip
Crowds: The Bund fills up fast — aim to arrive by 9am. On weekends, Yu Garden hits capacity by 10:30am, so head there directly after the Bund with minimal lingering.
Weather
This Saturday: Partly cloudy, 24°C. Light showers possible after 15:00 — pack a compact umbrella. Morning light will be soft and ideal for photos at the Bund.
Getting around
Metro tip: All three transit legs use lines 1 and 2. A single-journey card costs ¥3–4 per leg. Alternatively buy a day pass for ¥18 if you plan extra stops.
Food & budget
Budget breakdown: Yu Garden entry ¥40/pp · Museum free · Xintiandi lunch ¥60–80/pp · Metro ¥10–12 total. Total est. ¥110–130/pp for the half-day.
Pro tip
Queue hack: Enter Yu Garden from the Chenghuang Temple south gate — it's 10 minutes longer on foot but typically has no queue, while the main gate can be 30+ minutes on weekends.
